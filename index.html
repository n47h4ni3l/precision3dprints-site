Material advice without the guesswork.

Choosing the wrong material can waste time and money. Tell us where the part will be used and we’ll suggest a sensible option.

PLAClean finish for display pieces, simple parts and indoor use.
PETGA good everyday choice for tougher practical prints.
ASABetter suited to heat, sun and outdoor use where appropriate.
TPUFlexible prints such as covers, bumpers, bushes and grippy parts.

Pricing that starts with the file.

3D printing cost depends on material, weight, print time and how much risk is in the job. These ranges are only a starting point.

Small parts

Often around $25 for simple pieces under roughly 50g.

Medium prints

Commonly $25–$60, depending on time, size and material.

Large or difficult prints

Quoted individually, especially when supports, strength or long print times are involved.

Repeat parts

Small-batch pricing can reduce the per-unit cost when the same part is printed multiple times.
