FAQs

What files do you accept?

STL, STEP, and 3MF are best. You can also upload a ZIP containing multiple files.

How fast is turnaround?

Quotes are typically provided within 12 hours (business hours). Print time depends on size and queue — urgent jobs can often be prioritised.

Which material should I choose?

If you’re unsure, select No preference and describe the intended use in Notes (heat, outdoor use, flexibility, strength). We’ll recommend the best option (PLA / PETG / ASA / TPU).

Do you ship Australia-wide?

We offer free shipping. Choose Shipping in the quote form. We only request a full shipping address after you approve the quote.

Can you fix or edit my model?

This is a print-only service from customer-supplied files. If your file needs changes, mention what you need in Notes and we’ll tell you what’s possible or recommend the fastest next step.
