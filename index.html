FAQs

What files do you accept?

STL, STEP, and 3MF are ideal. You can also send ZIP bundles if your model includes multiple parts.

How fast is turnaround?

Most quotes are returned within 12 hours. Print turnaround depends on size, material, and queue — we’ll confirm timing with your quote.

Which material should I choose?

If you’re not sure, choose “No preference” and tell us the intended use — we’ll recommend the best option (PLA/PETG/ASA/TPU).

Do you ship Australia-wide?

Yes. Pickup is available in Adelaide, and we can ship anywhere in Australia once your quote is approved.

Can you fix or edit my model?

Basic checks and small printability suggestions are fine. For bigger CAD changes, include details in the notes and we’ll tell you what’s possible.
